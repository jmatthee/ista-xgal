Jorryt Matthee
Curriculum Vitae (15 September 2023)
Publication list (NASA ADS)

I am an Assistant-Professor at the Institute of Science and Technology Austria and lead a research group on observational extragalactic astrophysics. Before this, I was a Zwicky Prize Postdoctoral Fellow at ETH Zurich. I received my PhD in Astronomy from Leiden University in 2018, my MSc in Astronomy in 2014 and my BSc in Liberal Arts & Sciences at Utrecht University in 2012.
