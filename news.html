Latest News

Little Red Dot paper accepted [01/2024]

Our paper presenting the Little Red Dots: an abundant population of faint broad Halpha line AGN at z~5 has been accepted for publication in ApJ. Stay tuned for our press release, but already see discussion in Quanta and Nature.

Ivan's paper accepted [01/2024]

Ivan Kramarenko's paper (based on research during his MSc at the University of Geneva) on the relation between UV spectral properties and Lyman Continuum escape based on VLT/MUSE data has been published in A&A.

ALT observations completed! [12/2023]

The 48 hours of JWST observations for our 'All the Little Things (ALT)' have been completed. Stay tuned for results from NIRCam grism spectroscopy on the Abell 2744 lensing cluster field!

EIGER results listed among breakthroughs of the year [12/2023]

The magazine Physics World listed the first results from our EIGER survey as one of the Top 10 Breakthroughs in Physics in 2023. You can read the details on how we found direct evidence for local reionization by galaxies here.
